Step-by-Step First Aid

  1. Stay calm: Keep yourself and the injured person calm.
  2. Apply pressure: Use a clean cloth or gauze to stop bleeding.
  3. Clean the wound: Use clean water to remove dirt.
  4. Apply antiseptic: Prevent infection.
  5. Cover with bandage: Protect the wound.
  6. Seek help: If bleeding is heavy or does not stop.

Following these steps correctly can prevent serious complications.
