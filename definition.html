Definition & Causes

Definition: Cuts are injuries where the skin is broken due to contact with sharp or rough objects. Bleeding occurs when blood vessels are damaged.

Types of Injuries:

  • Minor cuts – small and shallow
  • Deep cuts – may need medical help
  • Abrasions – scraping of skin
  • Lacerations – irregular tears in the skin

Causes:

  • Sharp objects (glass, knives, scissors)
  • Falls and accidents
  • Sports injuries
  • Unsafe environments

Understanding the causes helps us prevent injuries and respond quickly.
