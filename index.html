First Aid: Cuts & Bleeding

First aid is the immediate care given to a person who is injured before professional medical help arrives. Understanding how to respond to cuts and bleeding is very important because it can prevent infection, reduce pain, and in serious cases, save lives.

This website will help you understand causes, symptoms, correct first aid steps, and how to prevent injuries.
