Signs & Symptoms

  • Visible bleeding (light or heavy)
  • Pain at the injured area
  • Swelling
  • Redness around the wound
  • Possible signs of infection (pus, warmth, increased pain)

Recognizing these symptoms helps in giving the correct first aid quickly.
